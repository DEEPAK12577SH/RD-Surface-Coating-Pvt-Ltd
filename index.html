Sandblasting services

+

ByDeepak Sharma

InWeb Developer

Sandblasting is also known as abrasive blasting. Basically, it is the operation of forcibly propelling a stream of abrasive material against a surface. The sandblasting operation is done under high pressure to smooth a rough surface, roughen a smooth/Shape the surface to remove its contaminants. There are several variants of sandblasting process like bead blasting, Soda blasting and shot blasting. Before sandblasting, it is mandatory to know about its materials and the pros and cons.
